BCA Scholarship Program

Providing a scholarship to 90 girls for the BCA program at Eternal University in collaboration with Navgurukul and Sri Badrika Ashram.

About the Scholarship

This initiative aims to empower 90 girls by providing them with a full scholarship to pursue the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program at Eternal University. This program is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the tech industry.

Program Details

The BCA program covers a wide range of subjects including programming, database management, web development, and software engineering. The curriculum is designed to provide hands-on learning experiences and prepare students for successful careers in technology.
